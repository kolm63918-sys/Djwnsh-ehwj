Meet Joe & The Story Behind 11th Street

Since opening our doors in **2007**, 11th Street Auto Service has been a staple of the Sheboygan, WI community. Founded and run by owner and lead mechanic **Joe**, our shop was built on a very simple business model: treat customers like family, keep pricing fair, and always provide honest, straightforward advice.

As a proud **NAPA AutoCare Center**, we guarantee our parts and diagnostics back up our workmanship. We pride ourselves on clear, straightforward explanations rather than high-pressure sales pitches. If you don't need a repair, we'll let you know. If you do, we'll give you a reasonable quote and complete it properly the first time.
